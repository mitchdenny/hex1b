xterm.js ConPTY Prompt Repro

This page uses xterm.js only. There is no websocket transport and no live Hex1b PTY here. It replays the exact PowerShell/ConPTY startup and clear redraw chunks captured from the browser demo. If the prompt disappears here too, that strongly points to a client-side xterm/ConPTY rendering issue rather than a Hex1b transport bug.
